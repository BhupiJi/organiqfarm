Drone Swarms vs. Herbicides

One of the biggest arguments against organic farming was weed control. "You can't hand-pull weeds on 1,000 acres," critics said. They were right. But in 2026, we don't use hands. We use solar-powered laser weeding drones.

These autonomous units patrol the fields 24/7, using computer vision to identify invasive species and zapping them with a micro-dose of thermal energy. No chemicals. No tilling. Just pure physics and code.
