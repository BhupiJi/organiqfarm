Sustainable Farming Practices

Published: December 22, 2025 | Author: Oraganiq Team

Sustainable farming ensures long-term productivity while protecting the environment. Here's how to implement it.

Crop Rotation

Rotate crops to maintain soil nutrients and break pest cycles.

Conservation Tillage

Reduce soil disturbance to preserve structure and organic matter.

Integrated Pest Management

Use biological controls and monitoring instead of chemicals.

Water Management

Implement drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting for efficiency.

Adopting these practices leads to healthier farms and ecosystems. See our resources for data.
