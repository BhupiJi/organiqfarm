Bio-Availability of Heirloom Crops

Published Jan 22, 2026 • 6 Min Read

Modern industrial farming has prioritized yield over density. In 2026, we are seeing the "Hidden Hunger" crisis—where people eat enough calories but are starving for minerals. Our study on Heirloom seeds shows a staggering difference in magnesium and zinc absorption compared to store-bought variants.

Why Heirloom Matters

Heirloom varieties have deeper root structures. They have evolved over centuries to thrive in organic ecosystems without the aid of quick-release synthetic fertilizers.
