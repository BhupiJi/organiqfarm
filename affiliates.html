Our Partnership Standards

We maintain strict criteria for affiliate partnerships to ensure alignment with our mission and values:

Certified Organic

Products must be certified organic or from companies actively transitioning.

No Greenwashing

Companies must demonstrate real environmental commitments, not just marketing claims.

Ethical Labor Practices

Fair wages and safe working conditions throughout the supply chain.

Transparent Sourcing

Clear information about product origins and manufacturing processes.

No Conflict of Interest

We don't partner with synthetic pesticide or fertilizer manufacturers.

Continuous Improvement

Partners must show commitment to reducing environmental impact over time.
