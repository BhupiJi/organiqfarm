Our Mission

To accelerate the global transition to verifiable, sustainable organic agriculture through open data, public education, and community funding.

Our Story

Founded by a team of agricultural scientists, environmentalists, and data analysts, OrganiqFarm emerged from a shared concern about the growing disconnect between food production and environmental sustainability.

We witnessed firsthand how conventional farming practices were degrading soil health, reducing biodiversity, and compromising food quality. Yet, we also saw the transformative potential of organic methods when combined with modern scientific understanding.

What started as a small research project has grown into a comprehensive platform that brings together farmers, researchers, consumers, and policymakers around a common goal: creating a food system that nourishes both people and the planet.
