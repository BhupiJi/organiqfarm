Our Mission

"To accelerate the global transition to verifiable, sustainable organic agriculture by 2026 through open data, public education, and community funding."

Who We Are

OrganiqFarm started as a small blog and has grown into a data-hub for the 2026 agricultural revolution. We bridge the gap between academic research and the dirt-under-fingernails reality of farming.

Our Model

  • Affiliate Partnerships: We recommend only tools we test.
  • Community Donations: Funding specific equipment for farmers.
  • Open Data: All our weather and soil findings are public.
