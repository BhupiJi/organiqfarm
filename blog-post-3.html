IoT Sensors in Organic Fields

Published Feb 05, 2026 • 7 Min Read

At OrganiqFarm, "organic" doesn't mean "old-fashioned." In 2026, we utilize a mesh network of solar-powered IoT sensors that monitor soil moisture, nitrate levels, and microbial activity in real-time. This allows us to apply water with 99% precision, ensuring zero waste.

Data-Driven Nature

By listening to the soil's digital pulse, we can predict pest cycles before they happen, allowing our team to deploy biological controls (like ladybugs) at the perfect moment.
