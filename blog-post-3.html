Organic Pest Control

Published: December 22, 2025 | Author: Oraganiq Team

Keep your organic farm pest-free without harmful chemicals. Explore natural methods below.

Beneficial Insects

Attract ladybugs and lacewings to eat aphids.

Homemade Sprays

Use garlic or soap sprays for mild infestations.

Physical Barriers

Row covers and nets prevent insect access.

Affiliate: Row Covers on Amazon.

Cultural Practices

Clean up debris and rotate crops to disrupt pest lifecycles.

These techniques ensure safe, effective control. Check resources for more tips.
