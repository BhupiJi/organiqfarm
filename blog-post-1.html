Starting Your Organic Garden

Published: December 22, 2025 | Author: Oraganiq Team

Embarking on an organic gardening journey is rewarding and eco-friendly. This guide covers the basics to get you started.

Step 1: Choose Your Location

Select a spot with at least 6-8 hours of sunlight daily. Ensure good drainage to prevent waterlogging.

Step 2: Prepare the Soil

Test your soil pH (aim for 6.0-7.0). Add compost to improve fertility. Avoid chemical fertilizers.

Affiliate Recommendation: Soil Test Kit on Amazon.

Step 3: Select Plants

Start with easy crops like tomatoes, lettuce, and herbs. Use organic seeds or seedlings.

Step 4: Maintenance Tips

  • Water deeply but infrequently.
  • Mulch to retain moisture.
  • Monitor for pests naturally.
