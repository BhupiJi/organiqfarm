Composting on a small farm

Jan 2025 • by OrganiqFarm

Why composting matters

Composting returns nutrients to the soil and reduces waste. On small farms, composting is the easiest way to build fertility without buying expensive inputs.

Materials

  • Crop residues
  • Manure
  • Green leaves

Basic method

  1. Layer coarse residues and greens
  2. Maintain moisture and turn weekly
  3. Use when dark and crumbly
