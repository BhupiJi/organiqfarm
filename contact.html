Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access your research data?

All our published research is available for free in our downloads section. For raw data sets, please contact our research team with your specific requirements.

Do you provide farming consultations?

Yes, we offer free initial consultations for farmers transitioning to organic methods. Contact us to schedule a call with one of our agricultural experts.

Can I volunteer for research projects?

We welcome volunteers for field research and data collection. Please send your background and interests to our research email address.

How do I cite your research?

All our publications include proper citation formats. You can find citation information on each research paper's download page.
