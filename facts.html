Verified Facts About Organic Farming

Data-driven insights backed by scientific research

Water Conservation

Organic farms use 30-50% less water per hectare compared to conventional farms through improved soil structure and water retention.

Source: Journal of Sustainable Agriculture, 2023

Biodiversity Boost

Organic farms support 50% more plant, insect, and bird species than conventional farms, creating healthier ecosystems.

Source: Nature Sustainability, 2023

Soil Health

Organic soils contain 20-40% more organic matter, improving fertility and carbon sequestration by 15-28%.

Source: Soil Science Society, 2023

Nutrition Density

Organic produce contains 18-69% higher concentrations of antioxidants compared to conventionally grown equivalents.

Source: British Journal of Nutrition, 2023

Farmer Health

Organic farmers report 40% lower rates of pesticide-related health issues and higher job satisfaction.

Source: Journal of Rural Health, 2023

Economic Viability

Organic farms achieve 22-35% higher profit margins due to premium pricing and lower input costs over time.

Source: Agricultural Economics, 2023
