Organic Farming Resources

A collection of tips, data, and guides to help you succeed in organic agriculture.

Top 10 Organic Farming Tips

  • Use compost to enrich soil naturally.
  • Practice crop rotation to prevent soil depletion.
  • Employ companion planting for natural pest control.
  • Harvest rainwater for sustainable irrigation.
  • Choose heirloom seeds for biodiversity.
  • Monitor soil pH regularly.
  • Avoid synthetic pesticides; use neem oil instead.
  • Encourage beneficial insects like ladybugs.
  • Mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
  • Keep detailed records of your farming activities.
